# Privacy Policy for Speech

Last updated: March 2026

Thank you for using Speech.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Speech mobile application ("the App") handles user data.

## 1. Data Collection

Speech is designed to work fully on-device.

The App may access:

* Microphone audio input for real-time speech transcription
* Device storage for saving transcriptions locally
* Basic device information (such as device model or OS version) for performance optimization and crash diagnostics

Speech does **not collect, store, or transmit personal data** such as name, email address, location, contacts, or identifiers.

## 2. Audio Processing

All speech recognition and transcription processing happens locally on the user’s device.

Audio recordings are **not sent to any server** and are not shared with the developer or any third parties.

## 3. Data Storage

* Transcriptions are saved only on the user’s device.
* Users can delete saved transcriptions at any time within the App or by uninstalling the App.
* The developer does not have access to any user recordings or text data.

## 4. Permissions

Speech requests only the permissions necessary for core functionality:

* Microphone permission – to capture speech input
* Notification enable - to notify running app in background

Users can revoke these permissions at any time from device settings.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Speech does not use advertising SDKs, analytics services, or cloud processing services.

The App may rely on standard Android system components required for normal operation.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Speech is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

Since the App does not collect personal data, it poses minimal privacy risk.

## 7. Security

All processing and storage happen locally on the device.
Users are responsible for securing their own device.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future.
Updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date.
